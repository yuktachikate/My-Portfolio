About

Hello! My name is Yukta Chikate. I am a masters Student at the College of Engineering Department, Northeastern University. Before this I worked at Barclays, India as a Sanction Developer. I am a part of a Quality assurance (QA) team and is working on Oracle Watchlist Screening (OWS) technology.

My work mostly includes defining requirements, designing, implementing, testing and delivering complexing back-end and web applications using variety programming languages and technologies. I have completed my research internship at CSIR- National Environmental Engineering Research Institute (NEERI). The research focuses on the Computation of GLDAS data using advanced Python programming which included detailed calculation and analysis using ArcGis software. I completed my Bachelor of Technology in Engineering in the year 2020 from the Department of Computer Technology at Yeshwantrao Chavan College of Engineering, Nagpur.

I am passionate about exploring different technologies and combining them together to make a useful product. Technologies and Tools Expertise: Programming Languages- C, C++, Python, React.js, Redux Tools- ArcMaps, OWSM, Microsoft Office
